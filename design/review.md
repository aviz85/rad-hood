# review.md — Head Comedy Editor: Punch-Up Notes per Scene

Read this AFTER story.md, jokes.md, looks.md and the voice docs, BEFORE writing your
scene file. These are binding punch-up notes: which strong joke goes where, which
moment to sharpen, which line is non-negotiable. Doc in English; quoted game lines
are Hebrew (feminine address, arrows `←` only).

Global notes for everyone:
- The funniest beat in any exchange is the LAST line. End on the punchline, not on
  exposition. If a g.say block ends with information, reorder it.
- One fourth-wall joke per scene visit, maximum. Pick the best one, cut the rest.
- Every death line from jokes.md Section B goes into `g.die()` verbatim — they are
  already punched up; do not "improve" them.
- The two-beat look formula (looks.md) is mandatory. A look with two punchlines or
  two setups will be sent back.

## scene01 — Red's home

1. The Narrator's 5-line opening (story.md §5) is canonical — use verbatim. The
   strongest line is #2 («וכשתמותי בצורה מטופשת — אני איהנה. זה בחוזה שלי.») — do not
   bury it by adding lines around it. Five lines, then control.
2. Mom's repeat-talk MUST land the dead-phone plant with her sarcasm intact:
   «...לא טעון. יופי. ככה תתקשרי אליי אם יקרה משהו, בכוח המחשבה.» This is a 12-scene
   setup; it only pays off in scene13 if the phone joke is memorable here. Also use
   voice-npcs Mom line 7 («קפוצ'ון זה אופנה, סוודר זה אמא») on a second repeat — it's
   the best Mom joke in the bank.
3. look photo must end on the chill, not a gag: «...כאילו צילמו אותה רגע לפני שהיא
   נזכרה במשהו מצחיק שרק היא יודעת.» Resist adding a punchline after it — the silence
   IS the joke. Same for look tv: «שלוש שנים אותה כתבה. אף פעם אין תמונות.» — stop there.
